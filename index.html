Door

The door in the house the player starts in is something made with the thought of an automatic opening process. There is a trigger in the form of a cube with a box collider that is invisible for the player, but will make it so the door opens once the player walks in the area the cube resides over. This method of triggers makes other interactions possible within the game.

Sword

The sword can be picked up with both your left hand and right hand. When the sword is picked up by the robot, the robot can either swing the sword or throw the sword to inflict damage on the zombies.

About

We created a first-person VR action game in which the player survives against a continuously spawning horde of zombies.  The player begins the game with 25 health.  Our fully animated zombies pursue the player using a navigation mesh and attack once in range, reducing the player’s health by 1.  If the player reaches 0 health, the game application quits.

To avoid damage from zombies, the player must move around.  The player character has a full animation set for walking forward and backward, as well as turning.  The character’s feet have inverse kinematics, allowing them to touch the ground whenever desired, including on uneven terrain.

To fight zombies, the player can use a sword, which has full physics via a rigidbody. Both the player’s hands and the sword also have trigger colliders.  On contact between a hand collider and a sword collider, the player can grip the sword with a button on their controller.

The game takes place in a mountain valley with a village at its center.  The player character spawns in a house whose door is interactable; it can be opened or closed by standing near it.  The player can explore the hand-sculpted wilderness as far as the mountains.

Character

Our robot character features an inverse kinematic system that allows his limbs to respond naturally to the player's motions. Space Robot Kyle can track your hand movements, grip positions, and distance from the floor. When the player crouches in real life, you can witness our characters legs bending as he lowers himself towards the ground. Try walking around in the game and picking up the sword for yourself!

environment

We designed the environment as an artistic choice with the theme of a beautiful countryside with a medieval village. The player can walk in the environment without destroying the objects.

Door

The door in the house the player starts in is something made with the thought of an automatic opening process. There is a trigger in the form of a cube with a box collider that is invisible for the player, but will make it so the door opens once the player walks in the area the cube resides over. This method of triggers makes other interactions possible within the game.

Sword

The sword can be picked up with both your left hand and right hand. When the sword is picked up by the robot, the robot can either swing the sword or throw the sword to inflict damage on the zombies.

Zombies

Zombies spawn from a single template zombie out of the player’s reach, at any of 32 spawn points.  They pursue the player using a navigation mesh, and have animations for idling, walking, attacking, and dying.

Health

The player begins the game with 25 health.  When zombies are in range of the player, they perform an attack animation and inflict 1 damage.  When the player runs out of health, they die, and the game ends.
